What is Theology on Tap?

Theology on Tap is a casual event where a guest speaker
talks about topics relating to faith and morals.
We have a few drinks, mingle, and hang out afterwards.

Open to All

Theology on Tap is open to anyone and everyone, regardless of personal beliefs.

Meet New People

The guest speaker is only part of the event. There is plenty of time to have a few drinks and meet some great new people.

Living the Good Life

Learn about the fundamentals of Christianity and how to truly live the good life.

Admission

Admission is $10 per person per event, or $25 for a season pass.
We've got to pay for the beer somehow!
